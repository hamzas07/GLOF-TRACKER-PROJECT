## ðŸŽ¯ Problem Statement

Glacial Lake Outburst Floods are sudden and catastrophic events caused by the failure of natural dams holding glacial lakes. Existing systems are often:

* Reactive rather than predictive
* Hardware-dependent and expensive
* Difficult to visualize for non-technical stakeholders

**GLOF Tracker** addresses these gaps by providing a **predictive, visual, and accessible web-based solution**.
